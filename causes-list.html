Humanitarian Efforts

ZNation understands that basic struggles faced by people transcend religions, nationalities, and ethnicity. With a powerful sense of moral obligation towards communities, ZNation involves in various humanitarian initiatives to help people meet their primary needs.


Let’s Build Bridges

Let’s Build Bridges or Aao Phul Banaaen is a campaign that aims to motivate organizations to construct bridges in the disconnected villages of Pakistan beginning from Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province. ZNation organized and funded for a bridge to be built in a village in the province to initiate the campaign.

Helping Schools in the Times of Pandemic

The global pandemic saw a lot of kids in developing nations struggle to attend e-learning sessions. They lack basic infrastructures like PCs or Laptops. ZNation distributed laptops to children across three schools in Pakistan ensuring that the learning does not stop.

Building Schools for Girls

The initiative that is very close to our heart is the “Gender Empowerment” project. This includes building Girls Primary Schools across villages in Pakistan, giving little girls hope, and opening doors to their future.
