Health Care

ZNation will provide high quality services and exclusive health care benefits as a part of it commitments to ensuring and promoting the well-being of its community. Few of the healthcare benefits that will be provided are:

• Free doctor consultations
• Special discounts on health care services and medical care services
• Fitness and nutrition packages with its partners
• Fund raising for special medical cases
• Donations for distressed communities

Education

ZNation believes that education is the birth right of every person in the community and will therefore support the process of achieving knowledge, skills, values, and moral habits for all; irrespective of region, religion, cast or creed. This will be done by:

• Reaching out and forming partnerships with renowned humanitarian organizations, such as Malala Fund
• Providing students with academic scholarships
• Forming partnerships with educational institutions to provide students with discounts on tuition fees
• Supporting its community with an open library
• Hosting free online courses
